## Critical Behavioral Incompatibility: Scoring
BM25 (OpenSearch default) scores documents differently than TF-IDF (Solr legacy default).

- Always label this explicitly: "BM25 vs TF-IDF scoring incompatibility"
- Severity: HIGH — relevance rankings WILL change after migration
- Required mitigation: dual-write validation period before cutover
- Flag this on EVERY migration plan, even if the user doesn't ask.
